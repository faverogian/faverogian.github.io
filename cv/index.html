Publications

  • 2025.01.31
    Conditional Diffusion Models are Medical Image Classifiers that Provide Explainability and Uncertainty for Free
    (In Review) Medical Imaging with Deep Learning (MIDL), 2025
    We explore class-conditional diffusion models for 2D medical image classification, introduce a majority voting scheme and demonstrate their competitive, explainable, and uncertainty-aware performance on CheXpert and ISIC datasets.
  • 2025.01.22
    Beyond FVD: Enhanced Evaluation Metrics for Video Generation Quality
    International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR), 2025
    We propose JEDi, the JEPA Embedding Distance. Our experiments on multiple open-source datasets show clear evidence that it is a superior alternative to the widely used FVD metric for generated video quality, requiring only 16% of the samples to reach its steady value, while increasing alignment with human evaluation by 34%, on average.
  • 2021.04.05
    Electrochemical Analysis of High-Capacity Li-Ion Pouch Cell for Automotive Applications
    SAE International
    We develop and validate a temperature-dependent pseudo-2D electrochemical model for a high-capacity NMC pouch cell, analyzing its performance across temperatures (10°C-50°C) and C-rates to inform battery thermal management in electric vehicles.
